Who is Jack

A house for the unfinished —
not a brand, but a witness.

Jack Stallings is a writer, podcaster, and theologian of friction working at the intersection of grief, masculinity, and faith. His work lives in the spaces institutions forget — the concrete hearth, the porch argument, the letter that never got sent.

He is the creator of Muses & Roots and Velvet Kink Confessional, and the author of dispatches published through the Taste & See Jack newsletter. He is currently in the quiet, but the table is still set.
